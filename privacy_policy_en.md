# Privacy Policy for Slap It! & Slap It! Pro

**Last Updated:** April 2026

The Slap It! team ("we", "us", or "ThinkQuack Team") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what technical data we collect and how it is used.

### 1. Essential Diagnostics
We utilize Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous technical data when the App crashes to maintain stability. No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is collected.

### 2. Optional Analytics
In the Pro version, users may opt-in to anonymous usage analytics to help us prioritize new sounds and categories.

### 3. Device Sensors
The App accesses the Accelerometer to detect slaps. Data is processed in real-time and never saved or transmitted.

### 4. Contact
Questions? Contact us at the developer email listed in the Play Store.
